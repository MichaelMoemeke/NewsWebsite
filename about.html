ABOUT US

Our Mission

Promulgating the truth and ensuring that no story is overlooked.

With different information constantly being spread, it can be difficult to follow what is truly going on in the world. We seek to provide our users with a website affording general news stories, organized into specific categories such as politics, conflicts, and updates regarding the ongoing pandemic. The website pulls articles and stories from already existing and reputable news sources, curating an environment of information from multiple perspectives and viewpoints. We provide summaries of multiple articles and stories covering the same subject. In doing this, people can gather information from the top, most trustworthy news sources, and come to their own conclusions based on what they have read.
